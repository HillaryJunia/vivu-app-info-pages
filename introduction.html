ABOUT THE VIVU TRAVEL APPLICATION

Welcome to Vivu - your trusted companion on every journey to explore Vietnam!

Background

The global tourism industry is undergoing a significant digital transformation, where technology plays a central role. Smartphones have become indispensable tools, leading to an explosion of mobile travel applications. Today's users expect rich application experiences: a source of inspiration for destinations, personalized suggestions, community interaction, and smart support.

In this context, Vivu was created with the aspiration to become a high-quality travel promotion application, especially for the Vietnamese market with its myriad beautiful landscapes and unique cultural heritage. Vivu not only helps promote the country's image but also provides a useful tool for domestic and international tourists.

Why Vivu?

Recognizing the importance of applying new technologies, our team decided to build the "Vivu Travel Promotion Application" based on key factors:

  • Meeting Modern Needs: Providing a platform that aggregates information on locations and tours in an intuitive, easily searchable, storable, and interactive manner (reviews, favorites).
  • Superior User Experience with Jetpack Compose: Utilizing Android's most advanced declarative UI toolkit, Vivu offers a beautiful, flexible, and efficient UI, enhancing the user experience.
  • Robust and Intelligent Backend System:
    • Supported by a stable, secure Spring Boot API capable of handling complex logic.
    • Integrated with Artificial Intelligence (AI) via a Chatbot to enhance user interaction and support, especially leveraging the application's rich data source to provide suitable suggestions.

Vivu's Mission

Vivu's mission is:

  • To be a useful, inspiring, and supportive tool for tourists in discovering the beauty of Vietnam.
  • To effectively promote Vietnam's image, culture, and unique tourist destinations.
  • To apply and master leading software development technologies, creating a product with high practical value.

Key Technologies

Vivu is proudly built on a modern and powerful technology stack:

  • Frontend (Android App): Jetpack Compose
  • Backend (API): Spring Boot
  • Smart Feature: Integrated AI Chatbot

A Message from the Development Team

We believe Vivu is not just a technical exercise but an effort to create a valuable product that meets market needs. We hope Vivu will bring you wonderful travel experiences and new discoveries in Vietnam.
